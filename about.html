ABOUT

As a photographer, Robert makes socially aware images that document and highlight contemporary social discussion. His work tends to be based around personal studies of the consequences of current affairs on localities. His work delves deeper, to see what effect we as people have on the earth and the way we shape the world around us. Following information from public domains and the stories of the people involved Robert aims to produce photographs that are aesthetically pleasing but rich with matter.
