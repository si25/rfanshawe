BORDER

This set of photographs looks at the British coastline in Hampshire. The beach and the coast is effectively our border and it differs from the land borders you see around the rest of Europe. It caught my attention as to how this sea border has become so important in contemporary social discussion, but is so seemingly unguarded and open.

During a time of mass migration on the European mainland, with Britain being a desirable destination, I am looking at what my border is in a modern European country.
