ISLE OF WIGHT FESTIVAL 2016

I often see litter scattered along the roads in England, it frustrates and confuses me why people think to just chuck their rubbish by the road or in a bush. These photographs are looking at examples of flytipping in Bristol and Portsmouth. Fly tipping is an act with a motive that I don't understand and one that commonly goes unpunished. Large amounts of waste are continually dumped in the British countryside.
